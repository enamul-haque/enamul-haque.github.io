About me

Currently, I am enrolled in PhD program in the department of Computer Science at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, LA, USA. I work at Network Science Research Group under the supervision of Dr. Mehmet Engin Tozal . Before moving to UL, I spent one year at Computer and Information Science department at Temple university, Philadelphia, PA. I received M.Sc in Computer Engineering from King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals, Saudi Arabia. I worked on wireless sensor and robot networks as my thesis with Dr. Uthman Baroudi. I worked at Grameenphone IT (currently Accenture), a subsidiary organ of Grameenphone Ltd. before coming to Saudi Arabia. I grew up in Bangladesh and spent most of my time there to pursue my degrees (secondary, higher secondary, and undergraduate). I received my B.Sc degree in Computer Science and IT from Islamic University of Technology (IUT), OIC in 2009. During my undergrad studies, I worked on database performance tuning in mobile cloud platform.
