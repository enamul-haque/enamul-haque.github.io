I am a Research Data Scientist at the Spencer Center for Vision Research at Stanford University School of Medicine, Palo Alto, CA, USA. I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the department of Computer Science at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, LA, USA. I worked at the Network Science Research Group. (NSRG) under the supervision of Dr. Mehmet Engin Tozal. Before moving to UL, I spent one year at the Computer and Information Science department at Temple university, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. I completed M.Sc. in Computer Science from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, LA, USA. I also completed M.Sc. in Computer Engineering from King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals, Saudi Arabia. I worked on wireless sensor and robot networks as my thesis with Dr. Uthman Baroudi. I spent few a couple of years at Grameenphone Ltd. before starting grad school. I completed B.Sc. in Computer Science from Islamic University of Technology, IUT, Dhaka, Bangladesh. My current research focuses on the structural inconsistencies in bipartite graphs and applications of machine learning in healthcare.
