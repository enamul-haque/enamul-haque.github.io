About me

I am a PhD student in the department of Computer Science at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, LA, USA. I work at Network Science Research Group (NSRG) under the supervision of Dr. Mehmet Engin Tozal. Before moving to UL, I spent one year at the Computer and Information Science department at Temple university, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. I completed M.Sc in Computer Engineering from King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals, Saudi Arabia. I worked on wireless sensor and robot networks as my thesis with Dr. Uthman Baroudi. I spent a few years at Grameenphone Ltd. before rejoining academic path. I completed B.Sc in Computer Science from the Islamic University of Technology, IUT, Dhaka, Bangladesh.
